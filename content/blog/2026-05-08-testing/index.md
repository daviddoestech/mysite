Everyone knows that feeling.

You finish doing something and think:

> "Alright, one more thing and then I'll sleep."

Biggest lie ever told.

You open YouTube.

Then another video.

Then another.

Then somehow you're watching a guy pressure washing a driveway from 2009.

You don't even own a driveway.

After that you remember Geometry Dash exists.

"I'll just practice for 5 minutes."

Forty-seven attempts later...

"One more."

Another fifty-seven attempts later...

"I almost had it."

At this point it's somehow 2:48 AM.

Your brain has stopped working.

Your eyes are burning.

You accidentally open File Explorer instead of Chrome because your mouse just gave up.

Then Windows decides it's the perfect time to install an update.

Thanks.

Finally you crawl into bed.

You close your eyes.

Your brain:

> Remember that embarrassing thing from 4 years ago?

Why.

Then you randomly get the greatest programming idea you've ever had.

You either:

- forget it by morning
- write it down
- open VS Code

If you open VS Code, it's over.

You're not sleeping tonight.

## Moral of the story

There isn't one.

You'll probably do the exact same thing tomorrow.

I know I will.
